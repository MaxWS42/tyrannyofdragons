***The Cult of the Dragon***

Talis (the White)
	Previously the Cult's white dragon *wyrmspeaker*
	Could read minds and spent a while partially inhabiting members of the parties' minds
	Was helpful in opposition of certain members of the Cult, because she was displaced from her role as white wyrmspeaker by Severin
	Was killed, along with her dragon, at the lodge
{ #da3f41}


Varram (the White)
	The Cult's white *wyrmspeaker* who took Talis' place
	A short dwarf, often wearing purple, with a long flowing platinum-blonde beard
	Killed near the Hunting Lodge, along with Old White Death
{ #bd2adc}


Rezmir
	A black half-dragon, the Cult's black wyrmspeaker
	Directed the raid on Greenest and freighted loot North to Castle Naerytar and through the portal there
	'Killed' several times by the party, in Castle Naerytar (after which the *dragon mask* disappeared), and finally in Skyreach (where she led the Cult's forces)^b1fe06

Sandesyl
	An elf vampire who resided in Skyreach's central tower
	Considered Severin's new plan for the cult to be blasphemous
	Was paralysed with a stake in her heart & then decapitated, along with her spawn
{ #bc51ec}


Sirathis
	A Yuan-Ti pureblood dragonsoul
	Represented the Ebon Maw (black) vigil aboard Skyreach
	Was very loyal to Rezmir
	Killed in the purging of Skyreach
{ #c1780f}


Kay Elzar
	A blue dragonborn dragonsoul
	Represented the Storm Callers (Blue) vigil aboard Skyreach
	Gave the following accounts of the other dragonsouls:
		Sirathis: “top dog” scary, quiet, ‘dreadcaptain’ would ‘quake his boots if Kay wasn’t as strong as he is’ reported fighting style: double-bladed scimitar black green flame tinge, came with Rezmir
		Brask: ‘warlord’ not as terrifying in quietness but serious anger issues, strong and ferocious, don’t play cards with him, pyrotechnic, not close to Rezmir
		Othric: eldest, strange northerly accent, likes cold and drinking, silent
		Selmara: scary, manipulation not fighting, poisoned last soul, politician, quite new
	Killed in the purging of Skyreach

Selmara
	An elf dragonsoul
	Represented the Emerald Coil (Green) vigil aboard Skyreach
	Killed in the purging of Skyreach

Brask
	An orc dragonsoul
	Represented the Ashen Horde (Red) vigil aboard Skyreach
	Confident that he was the strongest: he gained his warlord monicker after his village was burnt by a dragon then, as the sole survivor and having watched his parents die he conquered the neighbouring village at age 8 and controlled multiple towns and villages while still very young
	Hailed from somewhere East. 
	He suspected that Selmara may have disposed of Rezmir..?
	Killed in the purging of Skyreach

Othric
	A Goliath dragonsoul
	Represented the Pale Talons (White) vigil aboard Skyreach
	He is loyal to Talis
	Killed in the purging of Skyreach
